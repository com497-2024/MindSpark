Welcome to MindSpark!



What We Do:


This is an educational website for 6th and 7th graders! With AI-generated based questions that come directly from their assigned textbooks, MindSpark is a useful resource that students, parents, and educators can rely on.

This project was designed by two Computer Science students as our capstone project aims to help students during their academic journey. MindSpark was made by students, for students!

For Parents/Educators

As parents and educators, they will be able to create an account for their student and will be able to monitor their student's progress 24/7. Some exciting features include:


  • 24/7 real time progress tracking
  • Able to view student's chatbot history
  • Able to update their student's textbook to the one they need

For Students

Students who are 12 years old or younger will have to enter a parent/guardian/educator's email to make their account. Students who are 13+ years old will be able to create their own account.

As students, you will have access to many resources that will help you with homework and studying:


  • 24/7 chatbot that is trained with textbook information
  • Chatbot that is available when you are stuck on a question
