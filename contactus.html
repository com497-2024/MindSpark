About Us

This is our Capstone project by Luis Ortiz-Vega and Tierra Doughty. We are two college students who are passionate about helping other students learn in a meaningful way.

MindSpark is an educational quiz and game making website that will allow students and educators to learn specific subjects using AI.

The integration of AI will help generate specific topics based on student and educator needs.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a fun, self-paced, and interactive learning experience for students studying various subjects. MindSpark empowers parents, teachers, or educators to create quizzes or games to help students learn.
