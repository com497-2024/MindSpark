About Us

This is our Capstone project by Luis Ortiz-Vega and Tierra Doughty. We are two college students who are passionate about helping other students learn in a meaningful way.

MindSpark is an educational quiz and game making website that will allow students and educators to learn specific subjects using AI.

The integration of AI will help generate specific topics based on student and educator needs.

It is important for us that the student succeeds in anyway they can while enjoying the process of learning.

We're passionate about making learning fun and engaging. That's why we created MindSpark, a website packed with exciting quizzes and games that transform knowledge into an adventure!

We believe that curiosity is the key to unlocking a love of learning, and that's exactly what we aim to spark on our platform.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a fun, self-paced, and interactive learning experience for students studying various subjects. MindSpark empowers parents, teachers, or educators to create quizzes or games to help students learn.

Here are some values that we bring with MindSpark:

  • Learning Through Play: Tired of textbooks? We transform complex topics into interactive quizzes and addictive games that make retaining information effortless.
  • Variety of Learning: Explore a library of content across numerous subjects. Whether you're a history lover or a science whiz, we have something to challenge and entertain you.
  • Learn and Grow with the Flow: Our platform caters to learners of any skill level. Take quick quizzes for a knowledge boost, or embark on epic learning journeys through our comprehensive game mode.
